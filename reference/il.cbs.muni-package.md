# il.cbs.muni: Utility Functions to Work with Israeli Central Bureau of Statistics Municipal Data

Read and harmonize the municipal and locality data files published by
the Israeli Central Bureau of Statistics (CBS). The published files
change their layout, sheet order and identifier schemes from year to
year, and these utilities work around those quirks to return tidy data
with consistent identifiers. The package also harmonizes the several
identifier systems used for Israeli local authorities and brings
together data points from different years and sources, and it includes
readers for the socio-economic and peripherality index publications.
